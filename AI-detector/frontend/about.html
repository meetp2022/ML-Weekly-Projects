About AIChecking.me

AIChecking.me is a simple, fast, and privacy-focused tool designed to help users evaluate whether a piece of text may have been generated by artificial intelligence. Built for students, educators, writers, and professionals, the platform provides probabilistic insights rather than absolute judgments, encouraging responsible and informed use.

Text submitted for analysis is processed temporarily and is not stored or reused. Our goal is to make AI detection more accessible, transparent, and easy to use while respecting user privacy and maintaining a clean, user-friendly experience.
