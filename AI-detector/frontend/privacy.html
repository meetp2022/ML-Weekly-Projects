Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

AIChecking.me (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to being transparent about how we process data.

We do not store or save the text you submit for AI detection. Analysis is performed on-the-fly, and content is discarded immediately after processing.

Text entered into the tool is processed temporarily for analysis only. Submitted content is not logged, stored, or reused for training or any other purpose.
